Welcome to the official website for Ocean Exploration. If you would like to discover information about the wonderful but also terrible things going on in the ocean, such as pollution, the health of our ecosystem etc, then this is the right website for you! We have a wide range of information to help you explore the depths of the ocean and discover the secrets of the sea.

Ocean exploration is essential for expanding our knowledge of the marine environment, addressing global issues like biodiversity loss and climate change, and informing conservation and sustainable management efforts.

Would you like to take part in protecting our ocean? If so then please do click on the 'about' link, it will provide enough information to inform you with what's going on and guide you to do the right thing.
